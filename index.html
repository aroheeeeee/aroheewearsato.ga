frequently asked questions!!

do I have to study??

nope! if you don't feel like studying, just sign up and take the exam!

will i get a medal without studying?

depends on the exam! keep in mind that you've already learned a lot of this material in latin class! some exams are easier than others, though.

which exams are the "easy" ones, then?

national latin vocab exam and national latin exam! (if you're a myth person, then the national myth exam might also be easy for you! i'm not tho... lacrimo...)

why are those exams "easy?" are they only "easy" if you're "good" at latin?

of course not! visit the exam pages (links in the nav) to learn more about why each specific exam is easy and who should take it!

what is the score cutoff for a gold/silver/bronze medal for ____ exam?

for most exams, they depend on the avg scores and are often different every year. visit the exam pages to find out the approximate cutoffs!

should I go to ludi/carcer/states/nats?

YES YES YES DO IT!!! more info abt jcl events and contests will be added later!
